ADROIT-FX TRACK

Adroit-Fx Track is an online application for tracking all trades. The application is meant to help you become a stronger trader. Essentially, it is an online trading database for keeping records of what happened during a trade. You may include market condition, the size of the trade, expiration time, prices whether or not were successful and even note your emotion.

Adroit-Fx Track is a log that you can use to record your trades. Traders use a trading journal to reflect upon previous trades so that they may evaluate themselves. You can use journals to evaluate where you can improve your trading. They are a useful form of record keeping. Adroit-Fx Track can help you refine your strategies based on learning from previous experiences. Just as a business owner tracks inventory, a trader should also keep up with their closed positions
